About Department

The Department of Computer Technology provides quality diploma education with strong focus on programming, practical knowledge and modern computer technologies. The department encourages innovation, technical skills and project based learning to prepare students for successful careers in IT industry.

Vision

To develop skilled computer professionals with strong technical knowledge and ethical values who can contribute to society and industry.

Mission

  • Provide quality technical education.
  • Develop programming and practical skills.
  • Encourage innovation and projects.
  • Prepare students for industry and higher education.
